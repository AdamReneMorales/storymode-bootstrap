Welcome to StoryMode

Interested in becoming a writer? Looking for an easy and intuitive way to organize your story? This is the place for you!

What you can do...

Create, Edit, and Assign Characters

In StoryMode, you will be able to bring your characters to life by creating unique character profiles with over 100 characteristics and traits to modify. Once the character is fleshed out, you can then assign the character to which everone of your stories you see fit.

Sample

Allow your locale to jump off the page.

Much like with your characters, you can create detailed and elaborate location spanning from something as small and a building to something as large as a planet. With an assortment of elements incompassing culture, technology, government, and more, you can bring you background to the forefront.

Bring it all together

Of course, as much as characters and locations are important to a story, it's all for not without an plot. With our story editing tools, you be able to make notes about the story, create a professional looking outline, and write your story! Along with these tools, you will have others that will make your journey much easier, such as, importing characters and locale directly into your story.
